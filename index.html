A Genuine Commitment to Washington County

Hayden Hill isn't just a candidate; he is a dedicated public servant who genuinely cares about the people of Washington County. Through his work for the county, he has seen firsthand the importance of steady, reliable, and transparent financial leadership.

He believes that the Treasurer's office isn't just about balancing spreadsheets—it's about protecting the hard-earned money of taxpayers and ensuring that the county employees who serve our communities are supported and valued.

My goal is simple: to do the absolute best job possible for Washington County. I want voters to know that this responsibility is sacred to me, and I will put forth the maximum effort to ensure our financial house is in perfect order.

Core Priorities

A clear vision for the financial future of Washington County.

Fiscal Transparency

Taxpayers deserve to know exactly where their money is going. I will ensure the Treasurer's office operates with open books and clear, accessible reporting so you can track your tax dollars.

Extreme Responsibility

Every single cent must be accounted for and responsibly allocated. I will implement strict financial controls to eliminate waste and maximize the impact of county funds.

Employee Welfare

A strong county relies on strong employees. I am committed to advancing policies and financial practices that support the hardworking individuals who keep Washington County running every day.
